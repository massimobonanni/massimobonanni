# Architecting Intelligent APIs: Unifying AI Services with Azure API Management
##  05/05/2026 - European Cloud Summit 2026 - Colonia
### Abstract 
Modern AI solutions often blend multiple services—machine learning models, cognitive APIs, and custom inference layers. Managing these distributed components efficiently, securely, and cost-effectively is where Azure API Management (APIM) becomes the hidden powerhouse.
In this session, you’ll learn how to design and govern AI architectures through APIM—from optimizing performance with caching and throttling policies to securing endpoints, enforcing quotas, and gaining visibility through advanced analytics. Through practical demos and architectural examples, we’ll explore real-world patterns for integrating AI workloads under a unified API gateway that scales intelligently and controls cost.
By the end, you’ll walk away with actionable insights on how to use APIM as the control plane for your AI ecosystem, enabling your services to be robust, scalable, and budget-friendly. Ideal for developers, solution architects, and cloud professionals who want to streamline their AI delivery without losing control of performance, security, or cost.
