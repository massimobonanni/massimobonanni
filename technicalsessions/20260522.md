# Onboard Developers in 5 Minutes: Modern Dev Environments with Codespaces
##  22/05/2026 - DevDays Europe 2026 - Vilnius
### Abstract 
A new teammate joins. You say: “Just click here and start coding.” No setup scripts. No dependency mismatches. No tears.

In this session, Massimo Bonani explores how Codespaces + Dev Containers streamline onboarding and collaboration in teams. We’ll build a shared configuration, test it, and discuss best practices for team-ready environments. Your future teammates will thank you.
