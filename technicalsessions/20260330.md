# Unlocking Custom AI: Fine-Tuning Azure OpenAI Models with Your Data
##  30/03/2026 - Adriatics Tech Summit 2026 - Sarajevo
### Abstract 
In this session, we’ll demystify the art of fine-tuning in Azure OpenAI and show how you can build AI models tailored to your organization’s unique needs. We’ll start by contrasting Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) with fine-tuning, clarifying when each approach is most effective. From there, we’ll dive into the fine-tuning workflow—covering model selection, preparing training data in JSONL format, and running the fine-tuning process on Azure.
Attendees will walk away with a practical understanding of how to structure their datasets, choose the right base models, and successfully deploy fine-tuned solutions. By the end, you’ll have the tools to elevate your AI projects from generic to personalized—unlocking advanced capabilities that directly align with your business goals.
